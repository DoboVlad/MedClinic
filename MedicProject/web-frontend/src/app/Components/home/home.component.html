A little guide to the application

  • Find the doctor

    • Enter the application.
    • Enter your personal data.
    • Choose your family doctor.
    • Wait for your doctor to accept your request.

  • Program easily

    • After your doctor accepted your request you can make an appointment.
    • Enter the APPOINTMENT section.
    • Choose the desired date.
    • Choose the desired time.

  • No additional costs

    • No additional fees are included.

Un mic ghid al aplicatiei

  • Gaseste doctorul

    • Intra in aplicatie.
    • Introdu datele personale.
    • Alegeti doctorul de familie.
    • Asteapta ca doctorul sa iti accepte cererea.

  • Programeaza usor

    • Dupa ce medicul dumneavoastra v-a acceptat cererea, puteti face o programare .
    • Intra in sectiunea PROGRAMARI.
    • Alege data dorita.
    • Alege intervalul de timp.

  • Fara costuri suplimentare

    • Nu sunt incluse taxe suplimentare.
