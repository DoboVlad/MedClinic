A little guide to the application

  • Find the doctor

    • Enter the application.
    • Enter your personal data.
    • Choose your family doctor.
    • Wait for your doctor to accept your request.

  • Program easily

    • After your doctor accepted your request you can make an appointment.
    • Enter the APPOINTMENT section.
    • Choose the desired date.
    • Choose the desired time.

  • No additional costs

    • No additional fees are included.
